Our Ministries & Groups

Youth Ministry

Empowering the next generation with faith, leadership, and service. Our youth group meets every Saturday for fellowship, Bible study, and community outreach.

Meeting Day: Saturdays

Time: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Women of Fire Ministry

A supportive community for women of all ages to grow spiritually, build relationships, and serve together in ministry and outreach programs.

Meeting Day: Second Tuesday of each month

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Coordinator: Pastor (Mrs) Oyelade

Men of Valor Ministry

Building godly men through fellowship, mentorship, and service. We focus on spiritual growth, family leadership, and community impact.

Meeting Day: First Thursday of each month

Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Coordinator: Pastor Ezehie

Children's Ministry

Nurturing young hearts with God's love through age-appropriate Bible teaching, songs, crafts, and fun activities during Sunday services.

Meeting Day: Sundays

Time: During Sunday Service

Coordinator: Pastor (Mrs) Oyelade

Choir & Worship Team

Leading worship through music and song. Our choir ministers during services and special events, creating an atmosphere for worship and praise.

Time: 11:30 PM - 3:30 AM

Director: Mrs Yisa

Evangelism Team

Spreading the gospel through outreach programs, community service, and missionary work both locally and internationally.

Meeting Day: Third Saturday of each month

Time: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Coordinator: Pastor Yisa

Bible Study Group

Deepening our understanding of God's Word through systematic Bible study, prayer, and fellowship. Open to all members and visitors.

Facilitator: Pastor Ezehie

Join a Group

Ready to get involved? We'd love to have you join one of our ministry groups. Each group offers unique opportunities for spiritual growth, fellowship, and service.

How to Join

  • Visit our church during service hours
  • Speak with the group coordinator
  • Attend a meeting as a visitor
  • Complete a simple registration form

Benefits of Joining

  • Spiritual growth and development
  • Building meaningful relationships
  • Opportunities to serve others
  • Personal and community impact
